Our Services - Business Solutions

At Bolt, we understand that each company is unique and has specific needs. That's why we offer a wide range of customizable services to help you be more efficient and successful in your business. Whether you need mailing solutions, logistics services or added value, we are here to support you. Contact us by sending an email to envios@bolt.com.uy to discuss your requirements and find the perfect solution for you.

Email Solutions

  • Parcels - We deliver and pick up documents and packages throughout the national territory with several options to adapt to the needs of your company.
  • Messenger service - We offer various delivery options with different levels of urgency to ensure your shipments arrive on time.
  • Withdrawal - You don't have to travel to our offices; We go to where your merchandise is located.
  • Delivery confirmation - We provide standard delivery options, with signature confirmation or deliveries to specifically identified persons.

Logistics Services

  • Distribution - Expand your reach and reach more customers with our efficient distribution network that spans the entire country.
  • Consolidation - Optimize transportation costs by allowing us to consolidate your orders from multiple suppliers into a single shipment.
  • Storage - We have strategic storage facilities that integrate perfectly into your supply chain.
  • Supply Chain - Centralize your suppliers and improve the efficiency of your supply chain with our management services.
  • Enveloping/Packaging - Let Bolt take care of the folding and enveloping of your invoices, documents and advertising materials.
  • Labelled - Save time and reduce costs with our service of printing and applying labels directly to your products or packaging.

Added value services

  • Transfers - It makes money transfers nationwide at very competitive costs, with delivery times that in most cases are instantaneous.
  • Cash on Delivery - Facilitate collection for your customers with the cash on delivery service, managed by Bolt at the time of delivery.
  • Billing - We offer flexibility in billing and payment options, allowing you to bill the sender, recipient or third parties.
  • Tracking - Access real-time information about the status of all your shipments through our website.
  • Delivery confirmation - If you prefer, we can confirm the delivery of your shipments instantly by email or phone call.

E-commerce Shipping Service

We integrate logistics into the world of e-commerce, providing positive and trustworthy experiences. We offer integration solutions for Mercado Libre sellers, e-commerce platforms, management systems and websites. Our extensive coverage, first-class infrastructure, and competitive pricing make us a trusted choice in e-commerce shipping. We centralize the operation with full automated integration, providing timely information at each stage of the shipment. With years of experience behind us, we are committed to the continued growth of our clients in this exciting marketing channel.
