Bolt: Your Logistics and Shipping Partner

Bolt is a logistics and shipping company that is part of the QuickCarry Group, an entity with a solid track record in the world of transportation and distribution. At Bolt, we understand the fundamental importance of each shipment we handle, knowing that each package represents a promise and commitment to our customers. Our firm has become synonymous with trust and security in the field of logistics and distribution nationwide.

QuickCarry Group

The QuickCarry Group has experience spanning more than three decades in the logistics and transportation field throughout the Uruguayan territory. Our group is made up of several leading companies in the sector, including QuickCarry, ExpressCargo, SpeedLogix and Bolt. Together, we constitute a comprehensive logistics network that extends throughout the country.

We have a diversified fleet of more than 50 vehicles, which includes trucks and vans, supported by a team of more than 300 committed professionals and more than 30 branches strategically located throughout Uruguay. Since our inception, the QuickCarry Group has stood out for providing freight transportation services of the highest quality throughout the national territory.

Bolt: Efficiency and Local Commitment

Bolt is the answer to the growing demands in the field of logistics and shipping in Uruguay. Our growing network of operations connects cities across the country, providing customized and highly efficient delivery solutions. Renowned companies have placed their trust in us for the transportation of their mail, documentation and merchandise, and we are committed to maintaining and exceeding their expectations.

At Bolt, we are proud to have a team of highly trained employees who are dedicated to serving each customer's needs on an individual basis. We have implemented a continuity plan for eventualities that guarantees the uninterrupted provision of our services. Our diversified fleet and the use of multimodal logistics allow us to offer multiple daily frequencies to each location, even at night.

Our branches in each city are strategically located and offer extensive customer service hours. In recent years, we have achieved complete integration of our operations with various clients through advanced management systems, allowing us to effectively automate the shipping process.

At Bolt, traceability is essential. All our shipments have full tracking from the first to the last mile in real time, which allows our customers to know at all times the events related to their shipments. In addition, we have implemented the digitalization of signatures and geo-referencing of deliveries to guarantee greater transparency and security in each operation.

Bolt is more than a logistics and shipping company; We are your trusted partner to satisfy all your distribution and transportation needs in Uruguay. With a firm commitment to innovation and customer satisfaction, we are here to drive the success of your business through effective and personalized logistics solutions.
